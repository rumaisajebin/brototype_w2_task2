Welcome!



Tchibo stands for a unique business model. We operate around 900 shops in eight countries, about 24,200 retail depots and national online shops. With us, customers can discover really good things every day: coffee, the single-serving systems Cafissimo and Qbo, the weekly changing non-food ranges and services such as travel or mobile communications. With around 11,230 employees worldwide, we achieved a turnover of 3.26 billion Euros in 2021. We are the roasted coffee market leader in Germany, Austria, the Czech Republic and Hungary and are one of the leading e-commerce companies in Europe.
